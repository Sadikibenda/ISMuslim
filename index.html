About

"Our mission is to cultivate a vibrant Swahili Muslim Community grounded in the principle of unity, compassion, and service. We strive to uphold the teaching of islam while promoting a welcoming environment where individuals can grow spiritually, intellectually, and socially. through outreach, education, and charitable endeavors, we aim to positively impact both our community and the boarder society, embodying the values of peace, justice, and solidarity."
